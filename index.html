Kimchi

Kimchi [김치]

한국의 김치

Kimchi is a traditional Korean fermented food. It is made by mixing salted cabbage or radish with spices such as red pepper powder, green onion, and garlic and fermenting them.

김치는 한국의 전통 발효식품이다. 소금에 절인 배추나, 무 등을 고춧가루, 파, 마늘 등의 양념에 버무린 뒤 발효시켜 만든다.

Tteokbokki [떡볶이]

Tteokbokki is a popular Korean food made from small-sized garae-tteok. Eomuk (fish cakes), boiled eggs, and scallions are some common ingredients paired with tteokbokki in dishes.

Sundae [순대]

Sundae is a type of blood sausage in Korean cuisine. It is a popular street food in South Korea,generally made by steaming cow or pig's intestines stuffed with various ingredients.

Fish cake [어묵]

Fish cake is a food that is made like a rock by crushing the flesh of fish, adding salt, flour, and starch powder, lumping them together, and cooking them.

Bibimbap [비빔밥]
